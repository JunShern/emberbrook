Square pipeline

stage → derived walkability mask → prop library → composed scene (all one pipeline, no hand geometry)

1 · stage (generated free-form)

2 · walkability — green-fill derivation, thresholded + eroded (green = walkable)

3 · prop library (each style-anchored to the stage, chroma-keyed)

4 · composed scene (props placed on stage, y-sorted, footprint shadows) — with June for scale
